# Common Sales Objections — Sobriety Select

## Overview

This document captures the most frequent objections Sonia encounters when selling Sobriety Select listing tiers to addiction treatment facilities. Understanding these patterns allows for faster, more confident responses during discovery calls and follow-up conversations.

## Objection Categories

### "We tried directories before and didn't get ROI"
This is the most common objection from operators who have burned budget on Psychology Today or Rehabs.com listings without measurable census impact. The response centers on Sobriety Select's geo-targeted family-search positioning and the referral-tag integration that routes inquiries directly into their existing CRM (HubSpot, CallRail, etc.) so attribution is measurable from day one.

### "We don't have budget right now"
Budget objections frequently mask a timing or trust issue rather than a genuine cash constraint. A facility hiring intake coordinators or running Google Ads is demonstrably spending on census-building — the question is whether this channel earns its place in the mix. Anchor to the commission-only risk profile: the $600 Claimed tier costs less than one day of a paid media campaign.

### "We already have a full census"
Census-full objections are a buying signal disguised as a rejection. A full census today means the operator has validated demand — the listing protects future occupancy when census dips. Pivot to the Select or Premium renewal-value angle and the quarterly performance reviews as proof of ongoing ROI.

### "We need to talk to our marketing agency first"
Agency-gatekeeper objections are common at mid-size operators. Acknowledge the agency relationship and offer to include them in a follow-up call. Sobriety Select's tracking tags (UTM + phone swap) are agency-friendly and generate attribution data the agency can report on, making the listing a win for both parties.

### "LegitScript is too expensive / we're not certified"
LegitScript certification is required only for the Advertising / PPC service ($18,000/year). All three listing tiers (Claimed, Select, Premium) and Social Media Management are available without LegitScript. If the operator is interested in PPC but not yet certified, log it as a future upsell trigger and close the appropriate listing tier today.
