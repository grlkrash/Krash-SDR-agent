# Sobriety Select — Product Overview (2026)

## What We Are

Sobriety Select is a trusted online directory that connects individuals and families across the country with substance abuse treatment centers. We improve discovery for people seeking care and create fair visibility for providers.

## The Problem We Solve

Most treatment providers face concentrated visibility risk:

- Visibility and search rules are controlled by a small number of platforms
- Paid search has become increasingly expensive (e.g., "Drug Rehab Facility" YoY cost up 124%; "Drug Rehab" up 62%)
- Strong programs can be difficult to discover for the right reasons
- Providers without strong brand equity face concentrated risk

Location alone does not determine the best care. Most treatment searches begin with proximity-based assumptions. Proximity is often mistaken for fit, affordability, or quality. Important factors like environment, insurance, and outcomes get filtered out too early.

A change of environment can support recovery — familiar environments can reinforce old patterns. Rankings reward location, not outcomes.

## How Sobriety Select Works

### Map-forward discovery

Care is organized by region first, not keywords. This helps people orient before narrowing choices and reduces false assumptions early in the decision process.

### Designed for clarity

Only two primary choices to start the search: select insurance, select region, then browse. This reduces cognitive overload during a stressful moment, encourages exploration before narrowing options, and improves clarity for both seekers and providers.

### Regional organization

We organize care by region (Pacific Northwest, West Coast, Mid-Atlantic, Southeast, Southwest, New England, Mountain West, Midwest, Great Plains, Non-Contiguous) because:

- Geography shapes insurance access and provider networks
- Regional context helps people understand real options faster
- Encourages exploration beyond default proximity assumptions
- Leads to better-aligned inquiries for providers

### Profiles that go beyond a listing

Provider profiles provide real context, not just directory data:

- Programs can communicate philosophy and approach
- Strengths and differentiators are clearly represented
- Better context leads to more aligned inquiries

## Positioning for Sales Conversations

Sobriety Select works **alongside** existing marketing efforts — not a replacement for search, referrals, or outreach. It is designed to add stability across channels and improve clarity without bidding wars.

### What partnership offers (no pricing in email — save for the call)

- Enhanced directory placement to help facilities stand out
- Lead generation tools to connect with more clients actively searching for care
- Logo, photos, insurance details, and contact info in one place
- Verified reviews to build trust with prospective clients
- Map-forward, region-based discovery so families find fit — not just proximity

### Outcomes to emphasize (qualitative only — never invent metrics)

- More qualified families reaching intake
- Better-aligned inquiries because profiles show philosophy, services, and insurance
- Complementary visibility layer that does not depend on paid search auctions
- Fair visibility for providers regardless of brand size
